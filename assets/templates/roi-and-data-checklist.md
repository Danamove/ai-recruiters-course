## Part B: Candidate-Data Checklist

Run this for every role and review periodically.

### Collection
- [ ] Only collected data the role actually needs.
- [ ] All data came from free, public sources.
- [ ] No data behind a login wall or paywall.

### Storage and access
- [ ] Master sheet access is restricted (not public, limited people).
- [ ] No candidate data pasted into public tools or chats.
- [ ] Read my AI provider's data-handling policy.

### Compliance basics (GDPR and similar)
- [ ] I have a legitimate reason to process this data.
- [ ] I can find, correct, or delete a candidate's data on request.
- [ ] I have a retention window and delete data after it.

### Fairness
- [ ] Scoring uses job-relevant criteria only.
- [ ] No scoring on name, age, gender, photo, school, or background.
- [ ] A human reviewed every candidate who was contacted.
- [ ] Audited a sample for fair, explainable rankings.

### Truth and transparency
- [ ] Every AI step uses the "do not invent" constraint.
- [ ] All outreach was reviewed and sent by a human.
- [ ] I can honestly explain how I use AI if asked.
