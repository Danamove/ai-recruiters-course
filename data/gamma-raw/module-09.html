Two Final Jobs

Run It Safely

Candidate data, fairness, transparency. A system you cannot trust will not last.

Prove It Works

Measure the time and money saved. A system you cannot justify will not last.

Handle Candidate Data Responsibly

Collect Less, On Purpose

Public sources only. Minimize what you hold from the start.

Know the GDPR Basics

Lawful reason, transparency, minimization, retention, deletion.

Control Access

Limit who can see candidate data inside your system.

Never Let AI Invent Facts

Every data point about a person must be verifiable and real.

Keep Screening Fair

Score on Job-Relevant Criteria Only

Skills and experience. Nothing else.

Never Name Protected Attributes

No age, gender, school, background, or name in scoring logic.

Keep a Human on Every Decision

Watch for proxies. Audit occasionally. Fair screening is better screening.

Be Transparent with Candidates

"It helps me organize and prioritize. I make the decisions."

AI Helped Structure and Rank

The system organized and scored the pipeline.

A Human Reviewed Everyone

Every person contacted was reviewed by you first.

You Sent Every Message

Honest and reassuring. Candidates deserve to know.

Write the One-Line Business Case

"This system gives me back about N hours a week, roughly X per month, and lets me run M more roles at once."

That one sentence justifies the system. It is your pitch to yourself, your clients, and your business.

One sentence. Specific numbers. Written down. Done.

Sell the Outcome, Not the Tooling

Not This

"I will build you workflows and automations."

Say This Instead

"I will give your desk back N hours a week and a more consistent shortlist."

Price against the value, not your build hours. Clients buy outcomes. Lead with the outcome every time.
