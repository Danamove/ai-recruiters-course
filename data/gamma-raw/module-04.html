The Honest Tool Talk

Three tools dominate automation for recruiters. Here is how they compare honestly.

Zapier

Easiest to start. Gets expensive fast. Good for quick wins, not for scale.

Make

Visual and powerful. Cheaper than Zapier. A strong middle ground for most teams.

n8n

Best value and power. The builders' standard. This course uses n8n. Everything transfers to Make.

We use n8n in this course. The concepts are identical in Make, so your skills carry over if you ever switch.
