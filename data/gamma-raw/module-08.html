From Three Tools to One System

Before

The Operating System Framing

You are not running a screening workflow. You are running the system that runs your recruiting desk.

The Input

A role requirement enters the system.

The Output

A reviewed, ready-to-send shortlist comes out. That is the outcome worth paying for.

The Whole Pipeline, One Sheet

By the end, every candidate is one row with everything attached. Sourcing, scoring, and outreach drafts live in a single place, giving you a complete picture at a glance.

Step 1: One Master Sheet with a Status Column

Sourced

Screened

Drafted

Sent

Replied

Rejected

The status column is the spine. Each agent acts on rows at a status and moves them to the next. You always know where every candidate stands.

Step 2: Chain the Agents

Status-Driven Logic

Each workflow acts only on rows at its input status.

Screening

Processes rows with status: Sourced

Outreach

Processes rows with status: Screened, above threshold only

Simple, transparent, and easy to debug.

Step 3: Where the Human Stays

The agents handle the volume. You handle the judgment.

Add a Candidate

You decide who enters the system.

Set the Must-Haves

You define the criteria that matter.

Review the Ranking

You validate the agent's scoring output.

Review and Send Outreach

You approve every message before it goes out.

The Hire Decision

You make the final call. Always.

Step 4: Keep It Reliable and Simple

Fail Loud, Not Silent

Flag error rows. Do not skip them. A silent failure is worse than a visible one.

Spot-Check Weekly

Review a sample of agent outputs every week to catch drift early.

Resist Adding More

The smallest system that runs your desk well is the goal. Complexity is the enemy.

Sell the Operating System, Not the Wiring

Nobody buys three chained workflows. They buy the outcome: a role in, a reviewed shortlist out, in a fraction of the time, every time.

What You Sell

Lead with the hours returned. The wiring is your competitive advantage, not your pitch.
