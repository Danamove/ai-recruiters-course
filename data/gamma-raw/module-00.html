What Is an AI Agent?

Software that uses an AI model to do a task that normally needs a person to think a little.

It Reads

Takes in text, profiles, job descriptions, and context.

It Judges

Applies reasoning, not just rigid keyword rules.

It Produces

Something useful: a ranked list, a score, a draft message.

You Will Build Three Agents

Sourcing Agent

Finds and lists candidates. Free sources only. No paid tools required.

Screening Agent

Scores fit before you read a single profile. Saves hours of first-pass review.

Outreach Agent

Drafts a personalized first message in your voice. Every time.

The End-State: Your Recruiting Operating System

The machine sorts and drafts. A human always sends. That is a rule, not an accident.

Job Opened, then the Sourcing Agent, then the Screening Agent, then the Outreach Agent, then a Human Reviews and sends.
